Tarocchi

The word Tarot is derived from the Italian Tarocchi, and though the origins of the cards are disputed the earliest documentation of tarot packs are recorded from Northern Italy in the mid-Quattrocento, or the early Renaissance.


These early tarot packs would have been hand painted for the purpose of gaming, with little mysticism in the minds of their creators. It was not until the 18th century that the use of tarot decks for cartomancy became widespread, and a that they developed a new association with the occult.
